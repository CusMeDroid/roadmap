Roadmap

CusMeDroid is the only website that has grown on its single own, without a team, i can create a complete project. it's the way i create/write code without direction/different but at the same core.
i never expected more rewards for one page layout, but for more than one i was obliged to specify that amount.

in this github.io i would like to share with those of you who make projects without a team. with this roadmap i want to tell my goals, including :
- We are open source.
- Meet us in the search we made ourselves.
- We are free.
- Use our styles without the need to change them.
- And we are ourselves.


Note

Don't kill our characters by acknowledging our work, please appreciate those of us who want to inspire!
We allow you to copy all the contents of our project for you to learn and develop, don't just change the logo/images/url. Use your head to thinking!
