Roadmap

CusMeDroid is the only website that has grown on its own, without a team, i can create a complete project. it's the way i create/write code without direction/different but at the same core.
i never expected more rewards for one page layout, but for more than one i was obliged to specify that amount.

in this github.io i would like to share with those of you who make projects without a team. with this roadmap i want to tell my goals, including :
- We are open source.
- Meet us in the search we made ourselves.
- We are free.
- Use our styles without the need to change them.
- And we are ourselves.
